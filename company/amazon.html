Amazon

Explore Amazon’s work culture, technologies, and interview preparation tips

Company Overview

Amazon is a global leader in e-commerce, cloud computing, and AI-driven technology solutions. Founded in 1994 by Jeff Bezos, Amazon has expanded from an online bookstore to a technology powerhouse, delivering innovative products and services worldwide.

  • Founded: 1994
  • Headquarters: Seattle, Washington, USA
  • CEO: Andy Jassy
  • Mission: To be Earth’s most customer-centric company, where customers can find and discover anything they might want to buy online.

Job & Internship Opportunities

Amazon hires for a wide range of roles in software, cloud computing, data science, logistics, and product management. Internships provide students with hands-on experience in cutting-edge projects.

  • Software Development Engineer Internships
  • Data Science & AI Internships
  • Cloud & AWS Internship Programs
  • Full-time roles in Amazon Web Services (AWS), Alexa, Prime, and Supply Chain
  • Official Careers Page: amazon.jobs

Technologies & Tools

Amazon utilizes modern technologies across multiple domains:

  • Programming Languages: Java, Python, C++, JavaScript
  • Cloud Platform: AWS (Amazon Web Services)
  • AI & ML: Alexa AI, AWS AI/ML services
  • Big Data Tools: Redshift, DynamoDB, EMR
  • Development Tools: Git, CI/CD pipelines, AWS DevOps tools

Interview & Selection Process

Amazon’s hiring process evaluates both technical and behavioral competencies through a structured approach:

  • Online coding/technical assessment
  • Technical interviews (Data Structures, Algorithms, System Design)
  • Leadership Principles interview (behavioral)
  • Focus on problem-solving and alignment with Amazon’s Leadership Principles

Prep Tip: Practice coding questions on LeetCode and familiarize yourself with Amazon’s Leadership Principles examples for behavioral interviews.

Employee Insights

Amazon is known for its fast-paced, customer-centric work culture:

  • High focus on innovation and data-driven decision-making
  • Average rating: 4.2/5 on Glassdoor
  • Flexible benefits and employee wellness programs
  • Opportunities for career growth and internal mobility
