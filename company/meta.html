Meta

Explore Meta’s work culture, technologies, and interview preparation tips

Company Overview

Meta Platforms, Inc., formerly Facebook, is a global technology company focused on social media, virtual reality, and AI-driven platforms. Founded in 2004, Meta aims to build a connected world through its platforms including Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, and Oculus.

  • Founded: 2004
  • Headquarters: Menlo Park, California, USA
  • CEO: Mark Zuckerberg
  • Mission: Give people the power to build community and bring the world closer together.

Job & Internship Opportunities

Meta offers roles in software engineering, AI/ML, product management, design, and data analytics. Internships provide exposure to real projects and mentorship from experienced teams.

  • Software Engineer Internships
  • Data Scientist & AI Internships
  • Product Design & UX Internships
  • Full-time roles across Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, Oculus, and Meta Reality Labs
  • Official Careers Page: metacareers.com

Technologies & Tools

Meta uses a broad technology stack for its platforms:

  • Programming Languages: Python, C++, Hack, JavaScript
  • Frameworks: React, GraphQL, PyTorch
  • Cloud & Data Platforms: Internal Data Centers, AI/ML Tools
  • Virtual & Augmented Reality: Oculus, Spark AR
  • Analytics & Big Data: Presto, Hive, HiveQL

Interview & Selection Process

Meta’s hiring process is structured to assess both technical ability and cultural fit:

  • Online coding assessments or take-home projects
  • Technical Interviews: Data Structures, Algorithms, System Design
  • Behavioral Interviews evaluating leadership and collaboration skills
  • Prep Tip: Focus on coding practice, system design, and understanding Meta’s mission and values

Employee Insights

Meta is known for innovation-driven, high-impact work culture:

  • Collaborative and fast-paced work environment
  • Average rating: 4.3/5 on Glassdoor
  • Flexible work options and wellness programs
  • Focus on career growth, learning opportunities, and cutting-edge projects
